ABOUT THE KINGDOM

Kingdom of Ants is a highly interactive gamified platform that easily translates complex DeFi products into a fun, smooth, user-friendly experience. Instead of navigating wearily through covertly complex and difficult DeFi protocols, users can complete various tasks, including gathering food, participating in boss fights, and many other fun activities.
